* 18:30 – Turn up, have a drink, a snack, and a chat (food, soft drinks, beer, coffee)
* 19:00 – Why KISS and YAGNI Are My Two Favourite Design Principles - Mark Taylor
* 19:30 – A little break
* 19:35 – Rocking Out with PHP - Tom Wade

This is a **free event**, open to all. There will be some bottles of beer, tea, coffee, soft drinks, and food provided. We will have vegetarian food, if you have any other dietary requirements send us a message and we'll do our best.

The event will be located at [Platform above Leeds city train station](https://bruntwood.co.uk/our-locations/leeds/platform/).

# The Talks

## Rocking Out with PHP

### Tom Wade [@TomWade](https://www.twitter.com/TomWade)

> PHP doesn’t just have to be for websites. Resurrecting an old LeedsPHP talk favourite, we look at making PHP fun again.

## Why KISS and YAGNI Are My Two Favourite Design Principles

### Mark Taylor [@willCodeForAle](https://www.twitter.com/willCodeForAle)

> My talk will discuss why Keeping it Simple Stupid, and You Ain't Gonna Need It are my two favourite design principles based on my experience as a developer over the last decade or so.
